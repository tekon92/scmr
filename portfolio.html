PURI BOTANICAL RESIDENCE

PT. Copylas Indonesia is one of the Residential Division of PT Jakarta Setiabudi Internasional Tbk, a renowned Development and Property Investment company in Indonesia.

Puri Botanical Residence (PBR) is a residential consisting of 135 hectares located in the area of Meruya Joglo-South, West Jakarta. 53% of the area is designated in a residential area of approximately 3.000 housing units and the remaining area are used for public areas, including 6.5 hectares of exotic botanical gardens and commercial area along the side of the Jakarta Outer Ring Road West 2 (JORR-W2).

It is strategically located, close to public facilities and International schools /such as the national plus (High / Scope, the National High, Tunas Muda, IPEKA International, etc.) a variety of international standard hospitals (Siloam, Puri Indah), malls (Puri Mall, PX, Gandaria City, Mall Taman Anggrek, Central Park, etc.), shopping center (ITC Permata Hijau, The Food Hall, Ranch market, Giant, Hypermart, Carrefour, Lotte Mart Wholesale, etc.), and local markets.

We are the part of

Jakarta Setiabudi Internasional

PT Jakarta Setiabudi Internasional Tbk. (“JSI” or “the Company”) is one of the nation’s most prominent real estate investment and development conglomerate, which was founded by Jan Darmadi in 1975. Known for its service excellence and product innovation, JSI has successfully earned the trust of respected international business partners, including Hyatt International, Accor Asia Pacific, Itochu Corporation, Shimizu and Nomura. In 1998, the Company successfully completed its Initial Public Offering. JSI shares continue to be traded on the Indonesia Stock Exchange.
