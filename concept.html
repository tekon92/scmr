A design extraordinaire

The embryo of PT. Bias Tekno Art started from Santa Monica in 1991 where the first house was designed and constructed a year and a half later. The house got extensively published in both local and international publications (Japan, Singapore, America) and received the 1993 Ministry of PU (Ikatan Arsitek Indonesia) award for design excellence. Since then, over the years Bias Tekno has worked with and developed links with several local and international consulting offices in order to utilize their specialized skills for specific projects and also to maintain an international standard in our work.

PT Jakarta Setiabudi Internasional Tbk. has grown to become a premier developer in Asia. Driven by the relentless desire to deliver the highest form of function, design and enjoyment, we challenge ourselves to constantly think and see things from a different angle.

As we look towards the future, we remain focused on providing urban living solutions through our key businesses of property development and property fund management. All while we continue to create live-work-play environments of enduring value for the community
